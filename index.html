About Karnavati University

Karnavati University is a leading institution dedicated to excellence in education and research. Founded in 2012, we have established ourselves as a premier university in Gujarat, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

Our mission is to provide quality education that prepares students for the challenges of the global marketplace while instilling values of integrity, innovation, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in education, research, and innovation that transforms lives and contributes to the development of society.

Our Values

  • Excellence in all endeavors
  • Integrity and ethical conduct
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Inclusivity and diversity
  • Social responsibility
